Parallel Timer lets you run as many timers as you need at the same time. Perfect for cooking, workouts, studying, or managing multiple tasks.

KEY FEATURES

Run Multiple Timers
Create and run unlimited timers simultaneously. No more limitations on how many countdowns you can track at once.

Quick Start Presets
Launch timers instantly with your favorite durations. Create custom presets like 5 min, 15 min, or 30 min for one-tap timer creation.

Custom Timer Creation
Set any duration in minutes and seconds. Give your timers names to remember what each one is for.

Color Coding
Choose from vibrant colors to visually distinguish your timers at a glance. Perfect for cooking multiple dishes or managing different activities.

Smart Notifications
Get notified instantly when a timer finishes with sound and vibration. Never miss a completed timer even when your phone is in another room.

Undo Delete
Accidentally deleted a timer? Undo it immediately with a single tap. No more lost progress.

Dark Mode Support
Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition with full dark mode support throughout the app.

Bilingual Interface
Switch between English and Korean. Your preferred language is automatically detected.

PERMISSIONS

This app requires the following permissions:
- POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To notify you when timers complete
- SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: To trigger timers at the exact time
- VIBRATE: For timer completion vibration feedback

PRIVACY

Parallel Timer does not collect, use, or share any of your personal data. All timer data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required.

REQUIREMENTS

- Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher
- Approximately 10 MB of storage space
